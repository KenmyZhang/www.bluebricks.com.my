Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to pay any upfront fees?
We do not require any upfront fees, stamp duty or legal fees. Please inform our management team if any of our loan consultants have asked you for any upfront payment. We will report them to the police to bring justice to the victims.
Why doesn't Bluebricks collect any upfront fees?
Due to the many loan scams in the market today, we decided not to collect any upfront fees as we want our customers to feel safe while we work on their loan applications. You will only pay us once you get your bank loan.

Moreover, we do not want to cause more stress to our customers as they wait for their loans to be approved. Therefore, we only charge once you have received your desired loan amount.
How does Bluebricks make money?
We only make a profit once our customers have successfully acquired their loans from the bank. Thus, believe us when we say we are together with you to help you get the loan you desire.
Why was my loan application rejected?
Your loan application can be rejected for several reasons:
  • Bad credit scores
  • Insufficient income
  • High debt-to-income ratio
  • Missing key information or documents
What do I do if the bank rejected my loan application?
Not getting the approval for a much-needed loan is not fun, but consulting a loan agency in Malaysia like Bluebricks can help. We identify why your loan application was rejected and will make the necessary amendments to improve your chances of approval.
